Product Details


"Bleu de Chanel" exudes a blend of fresh, aromatic, and woody notes, creating a timeless and masculine scent. Opening with invigorating citrus notes of grapefruit and bergamot, it evolves into a heart of intense labdanum and jasmine, balanced with a hint of spicy pink pepper. The base notes of cedar, vetiver, and frankincense add depth and sensuality, leaving a lasting impression of elegance and allure. This fragrance is designed for the confident and discerning man who appreciates a blend of classic sophistication and contemporary freshness.
